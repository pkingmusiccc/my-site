# TAO Cosmetics — research notes

> Read-only reference. Source: https://www.tao-cosmetics.de (fetched 2026-04-23). Do not copy — reference only.

## Positioning

- B2B-only. **No direct-to-consumer sales**, no public online shop.
- Products are only available through partnered dermatological institutes and aesthetic clinics. Gatekeeping is the business model.
- Self-description leans **medical/dermatological**, not luxury-aspirational.

## Voice

- Formal, clinical, professional. Reassurance over persuasion.
- Recurring German phrases and their feel:
  - *individuell abgestimmte Pflegekonzepte* — customised care concepts
  - *neuesten wissenschaftlichen Erkenntnissen* — latest scientific findings
  - *hochdosierte Wirkstoffe* — high-concentration active ingredients
- Positions efficacy and suitability for sensitive skin above all else.

## Product taxonomy

Nine primary lines:

1. **Clean** — cleansers
2. **Light** — normal/combination/oily
3. **Dry** — dry skin
4. **Glossy** — radiance / mature
5. **More** — boosters / serums
6. **Body** — body care
7. **Sun** — sun care, notable Ectoin use
8. **Man** — men's line
9. **Epiceutical** — premium sub-brand (positioned above the main range)

Products are cross-indexed three ways:
- by product type (cleanser, cream, serum, mask, peel)
- by skin condition (normal/combination, dry, oily/impure, sensitive/reactive, mature)
- by body area (face, eye, lip, body, hair)

Professional-only categories include enzyme peels and fruit-acid peels.

## Credentials (facts to lean on)

- **Developed and manufactured in Germany** — *Entwickelt und hergestellt in Deutschland*.
- **26+ years** of operation. Timeline available on the site (*Chronik*).
- Products emerge from **dialogue between dermatology and cosmetics** — collaboration with practising dermatologists.
- EU Cosmetics Regulation compliant. **No animal testing**.
- **No paraffins, no mineral oils**. Nearly all products free of animal ingredients (exception: beeswax in a few for skin protection).
- **Sustainability**: Planet Tree partnership (tree-planting initiative) since October 2024.

## Visual language

- Minimal, monochromatic. White-on-dark brand lockups.
- Typography reads modern sans (corporate, not editorial).
- Imagery: clinical-professional. Partner testimonials are headshots with institutional titles, not lifestyle models.
- Overall mood: trustworthy, medically grounded, understated.

## Implications for Vitracosmetics (Swiss distributor site)

- **Diverge** on typography — TAO is sans/corporate; we go serif/editorial (Fraunces + Inter) to read more Swiss-clinical-luxe and less corporate-medical.
- **Converge** on restraint and clinical credibility. No luxury aspiration. No lifestyle models.
- **Lean on** the concrete credentials: Germany-made, 26 years, dermatologist collaboration, EU-regulated, no animal testing, no paraffins/mineral oils, Planet Tree partnership.
- **Do not copy** any TAO copy lines verbatim. Use the facts, write our own sentences.
- Product showcase can mirror TAO's taxonomy (by line → by skin condition → by area) since professionals expect that structure.
